Workshop on Solid State Physics

The school is oriented to the PhD students and young researchers specialized in optoelectronics, solid state physics, quantum electrodynamics, lasers, Terahertz technology, nanotechnology and related innovation. The school is organized jointly by IFMO University and Lappeenranta University of technology in near quiet Finnish lake Imatra.
